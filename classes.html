REGULAR COURSES

TECHNIQUE COURSE

THIS CLASS INCLUDES FOUR ASPECTS: STRETCHING, PHYSIQUE, HIGH HEELS AND FRAME UP FOUNDATIONS. THE EXERCISES GET HARDER OVER TIME, AND THERE WILL ALWAYS BE ROOM FOR IMPROVEMENT. IT'S AN IMPORTANT CLASS FOR ANY HEELS DANCER.

HIGH HEELS CHOREO

HIGH HEELS DANCE IS A CAPTIVATING FORM OF ARTISTIC EXPRESSION THAT COMBINES THE ELEGANCE AND GRACE OF DANCE WITH THE BOLDNESS AND CONFIDENCE OF WEARING HIGH HEELS. THIS UNIQUE STYLE CELEBRATES FEMININITY, EMPOWERMENT, AND SELF-EXPRESSION. THE LEVELS IN MY CLASSES ARE ADVANCED AND INTERMEDIATE, WHICH MEANS YOU SHOULD ALREADY HAVE SOME DANCE EXPERIENCE. HOWEVER, IF YOU'RE READY TO WORK, YOU CAN ALSO TRY OR CHECK OUT THE HIGH HEELS BEGINNERS SPECIAL COURSE.

FRAME UP STRIP

THE FOUNDER OF FRAME UP STRIP IS NASTYA YURASOVA. THIS DANCE APPEARED IN RUSSIA MORE THAN 10 YEARS AGO. FEATURES OF THE STYLE ARE: TECHNICALITY, PARTIALLY BORROWED MOVEMENTS FROM HIGH HEELS, BREAKDANCE, AND JAZZ-FUNK. MOST OF THE ELEMENTS ARE PERFORMED ON THE FLOOR WITH PROPER SHOES.
