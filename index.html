ABOUT VLADA KOSHA

STARTED MY JOURNEY AS A BALLET DANCER BEFORE FINDING MY TRUE PASSION IN HIGH HEELS, NOW HAVING 10 YEARS OF EXPERIENCE THERE. PUSHING BOUNDARIES AND REDEFINING DANCE, I STRIVE TO ELEVATE HIGH HEELS AS A PROFESSIONAL DANCE STYLE THROUGH TECHNIQUE CLASSES.

TECHNIQUE IS MY FOCUS, BUT IN MY CHOREOGRAPHY, I’M TRYING TO CREATE SOMETHING UNIQUE TO DISCOVER THE IMPORTANCE OF SELF-EXPRESSION THROUGH MOVEMENT AND EMBRACE THE TRANSFORMATIVE POWER OF DANCE. BEYOND THE STUDIO, I DIRECT CAPTIVATING VIDEOS AND CURATE SHOWS; YOU CAN FIND MY PORTFOLIO HERE.
