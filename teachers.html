VLADA KOSHA

Vlada is a founder and a choreograper of HHeels. At HHeels she teaching intermediate High Heels and Frame up courses.

She says: "In my classes you will have calm but productive atmospher. Teaching s my job, my hobby, my love and my passion, so I always very happy to share my knowledge with you!

Vlada has two educations: ballet dancer and choreographer. She's been teaching for about 17 years overall, the last 14 years in High Heels especially. She is always invest her time in education: courses, workshops and other events.
